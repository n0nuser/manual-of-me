Professional

My preferred channels of communication are...

In priority order:

Video/Voice Chat

  • It's the most personal way to communicate, and allows for the most context to be shared
  • It's the best way to build rapport and trust

Email

  • It's more formal and allows to locate the conversation easily
  • It allows me to schedule time to respond to you properly

Instant messaging

  • Give me a bit of context for what you would like (check nohello.net)
  • If messages are urgent/time sensitive, it'd be good to know!

I work best when

  • Tasks are clearly defined and work can be break down
  • I have music on!

What gives me energy at work

  • Feeling valued
  • Having supportive colleagues
  • Being able to help grow myself and others, to improve ourselves technically and personally (and being able to perceive it!)
  • Being able to have a good work/life balance
  • Having a joyful relaxed working atmosphere
